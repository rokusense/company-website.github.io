Save Dodger

Platform : Android

Dogs on earth are in Danger! Aliens are capturing all the dogs on Earth! Dodger the dashing Dog is captured by Aliens. Use your crazy high tech gun to destroy the Aliens and save dodger! Are you ready to blow up the aliens and save dodger? Goal: your goal is to earn more points by shooting as many aliens as you can.

How to play?
  • Tap on top left of the screen to go up.
  • Tap on the bottom left of the screen to go down.
  • Tap anywhere on the right side of the screen to shoot the aliens coming out of the pipe.

Connect Dots GO

Platform : Android

Amazing art style pattern making puzzle game. Touch the dots to make patterns within a given time to tests your brain capability.

How to play
  • Start by selecting the level.
  • Then in a stipulated amount of time, Try and make the pattern by touching the correct dots because each dot can be touched only once.
  • If the pattern matches you win and proceed to the next level.
  • If the pattern doesn't match, You can retry to make the correct pattern but each time you retry the level, 10 seconds will be reduced from the total time available for making the pattern.
  • To revive the lost time you can watch video ads at the end of every failed level.
  • The complexity of the pattern increases as you go up the levels.
